🍕 Homemade Cheese Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is a perfect combination of a crispy base, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese. It is easy to prepare and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp chili flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C for 10 minutes.
  2. Place the pizza base on a baking tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the base.
  4. Add chopped vegetables on top.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese.
  6. Bake for 10–15 minutes until cheese melts.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  8. Cut into slices and serve hot.

Tips

Use fresh cheese for better taste. You can also add corn, olives, or mushrooms.

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
