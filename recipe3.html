🍔 Classic Veg Burger

Description

This veg burger is crispy on the outside and soft inside, layered with fresh vegetables and flavorful sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 veg patty
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 1 cheese slice
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup

Steps

  1. Cut burger buns into halves.
  2. Toast them lightly on a pan.
  3. Cook the veg patty until golden brown.
  4. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on buns.
  5. Add lettuce, patty, tomato, and cheese.
  6. Close the burger and press gently.
  7. Serve hot with fries.

Tips

Add extra cheese or spicy sauce for more flavor.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
