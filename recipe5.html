Description

This healthy salad is fresh, light, and full of nutrients, perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 cup lettuce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop them into small pieces.
  3. Add them to a bowl.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Mix everything well.
  6. Serve fresh.

Tips

Add salt, pepper, or herbs for extra flavor.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
