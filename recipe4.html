🍰 Chocolate Cake

Description

A rich and moist chocolate cake that is perfect for celebrations and dessert cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs and butter and mix well.
  4. Pour batter into greased tray.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Check with a toothpick.
  7. Let it cool before serving.

Tips

Decorate with chocolate syrup or cream for better presentation.

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
